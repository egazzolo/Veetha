Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 30, 2025

Simple Summary: We use your camera to scan barcodes. We don't store your photos or personal information. All nutrition data comes from OpenFoodFacts, a free public database.

1. Information We Collect

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • Camera Access: We access your device camera only to scan product barcodes. No photos or videos are saved or stored.
  • Barcode Data: When you scan a barcode, we send the barcode number to OpenFoodFacts API to retrieve nutritional information. We do not store this information.
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

2. How We Use Your Information

The camera is used exclusively for:

  • Scanning product barcodes in real-time
  • Future feature: Analyzing food photos for nutritional estimation

All captured images are processed locally on your device and are immediately discarded after processing.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • OpenFoodFacts API: A free, open-source database of food products worldwide. When you scan a barcode, we query their public API to retrieve nutritional information. OpenFoodFacts does not track individual users. Learn more at openfoodfacts.org.

4. Data Storage

We do not store any data on our servers. All app functionality operates in real-time without saving:

  • Photos or camera captures
  • Scan history
  • Search queries
  • Personal preferences

5. Camera Permissions

Our app requires camera access to function. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → Nutrition Scanner
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Nutrition Scanner → Permissions → Camera

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require any personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as we add new features. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over camera access through your device settings.
